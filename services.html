FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How is VelintraLabs different from a web design agency?

Most agencies focus on design and development. We focus on business performance. Every website we build is designed to generate enquiries, not just look good. And we go beyond websites — we connect your systems, automate your processes, and implement AI where it creates real value.

Do I need all three services — website, automation, and AI?

Not at all. Most businesses start with one challenge and expand over time. We'll recommend exactly what you need based on your current priorities — never more. The goal is to solve your most impactful problem first, then build from there.

How long does a typical project take?

It depends on scope. A high-performance website typically takes 4–8 weeks. Automation projects can range from 2–6 weeks depending on complexity. We'll give you a clear timeline after understanding your specific needs during our strategy call.

What does the free strategy call involve?

It's a 30-minute conversation where we learn about your business, understand your current challenges, and share initial ideas for how we could help. There's no pressure and no obligation — it's a genuine conversation to see if we're a good fit for each other.

What types of businesses do you work with?

We work with growing businesses that have outgrown their current systems. This typically includes service businesses, professional firms, e-commerce companies, and established small-to-medium businesses ready to scale more efficiently.

Do you offer ongoing support after a project is delivered?

Yes. We believe in continuous improvement, not one-and-done delivery. After launch, we measure results, gather feedback, and make refinements. We offer ongoing optimization packages to ensure your systems keep performing better over time.
